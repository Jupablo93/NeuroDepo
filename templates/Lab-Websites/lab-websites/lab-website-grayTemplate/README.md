# Lab Website Template

A minimalist and modular lab website template ideal for research groups, academic labs, or faculty members.

## 📁 Folder Structure

/lab-websites/
├── category.json
└── lab-website-template1/
├── manifest.json
├── index.html # About Us (Home)
├── team.html # Team Members
├── research.html # Research Projects
├── publications.html # Publications List
├── events.html # Lab Events or News
├── contact.html # Contact Information
├── style.css # Common Styles
└── images/ # Team photos, logos, etc.

markdown
Copiar
Editar

## 📌 Pages

- `index.html`: Lab overview and mission.
- `team.html`: PI and lab members bios.
- `research.html`: Research lines and current projects.
- `publications.html`: Peer-reviewed articles, preprints, etc.
- `events.html`: Conferences, workshops, outreach.
- `contact.html`: Email, location, social media links.

## 🧩 Configuration

- `manifest.json`: Template metadata (name, version, preview, etc.).
- `category.json`: Organizes templates in the "Lab Websites" category.

## 🛠️ Customization Tips

- Add your logo and team photos to the `images/` folder.
- Replace content in each `.html` file to reflect your lab.
- Add links to GitHub, ORCID, or personal websites in the team or contact section.

## 🖥️ Preview

Open `index.html` in your browser to preview the site locally.
